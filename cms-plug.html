SVG Import: Say goodbye to the 10,000 character limit.

Bring any SVG into your CMS Full Form projects as native, editable elements with SVG Import. Say goodbye to the 10,000 character limit on SVGs in CMS Full Form. With SVG Import, simply paste your SVG code directly into CMS Full Form and let the extension automatically generate all the required DOM elements for you.

Here's how SVG Import enhances your CMS Full Form design experience:

  • Simple paste & go: Paste your SVG code and SVG Import takes care of the rest.
  • No More 10,000 Character Limit: Break free from the 10,000 character limit on SVGs in CMS Full Form. SVG Import allows you to work with extensive SVG files without a hitch.
  • Works with All SVGs: Whether it's animated SVGs, gradient SVGs, or ultra-super complex SVGs, SVG Import handles them all with ease.
  • Bind CMS Colors: Seamlessly bind CMS colors to individual shapes within an SVG, extending the dynamic capabilities of your designs.
  • Perfect for Designers and Developers: Whether you're a seasoned developer or a creative designer, SVG Import simplifies the SVG integration process, saving you time and keeping the focus on your design.
  • Maintain SVG Fidelity: Ensure your SVGs are displayed as intended with accurate DOM element generation, preserving the original attributes and styles of your SVG graphics.
  • Quick: SVG Import efficiently handles the DOM element creation, letting you design with SVGs effortlessly.
  • Supports all valid SVG Elements: From basic shapes, paths, to complex illustrations, SVG Import supports a wide range of SVG elements, offering flexibility in your design process.
